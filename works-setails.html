Selected Projects

Research • Engineering • Clinical

Here are a few projects and research themes that represent my recent work.

  • Protein Generative AI (Pfizer SGE) — Built protein generative-AI pipelines using ProGen1, ProtGPT, and RITA, with structure evaluation via AlphaFold2 and PyRosetta.
  • DYRK1A Trisomy & Visual Cortical Plasticity — Down syndrome mouse-model research on synaptic plasticity and cell-type specific mechanisms, including Arc in situ hybridization and pharmacologic rescue.
  • Youth-Associated Blood Factors & TIMP2 (Castellano Lab) — Studying circulating youth-associated factors and TIMP2/ECM microenvironmental effects using parabiosis, microfluidics, and perfusion workflows.
  • Soft Robotic Prosthetics & Flexible Biosensors — Developed silicone pneumatic finger prosthetics and thin, flexible pressure/electro-sensitive biosensors for wearable bio-robotics applications.
  • N95 Mask Reuse Disinfection Systems — Research on scalable disinfection systems for N95 reuse with real-world deployment goals; prior work reached 20k+ reads/downloads.
  • Retinotopic Visual Cortex Mapping (Winawer Lab) — ML-assisted cortical mapping and Docker-based pipelines generating dorsal/ventral visual cortex (V1–V5) maps from ultrahigh-field fMRI datasets.
